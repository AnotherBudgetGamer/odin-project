Lasagna

Home

Ingredients:
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Garlic
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
  1. Boil the pasta until it is about 1⁄2 way done. Very important the pasta sheets are not all the way cooked.
  2. Layer in the following order, repeating
    • pasta
    • sauce
    • cheese
    • sauce
  3. Top with Mozzarella Cheese
  4. Bake until done. Around 45 min
  5. Best served with Garlic Bread.
