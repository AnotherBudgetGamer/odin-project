Ingredients:
  • Bread Loaf
    • - French Bread is best suited for it's density and structure for toasting and soaking juices.
  • Extra-Vigin Olive Oil (EVOO) - 1 Tbls
  • Roma Tomatoes - 8
  • Fresh Basil - 1⁄3 Cup - chopped
  • 1 oz Parmesean Cheese - grated
  • 2 cloves Garlic - minced
  • Balsamic Vinegar - 1 Tbls
  • EVOO - 2 tspn
  • Kosher Salt - 1⁄4
  • Ground Black Pepper - 1⁄4 Cup

Directions:
Slice and toast bread using 1 Tbls of EVOO. The easiest way to do this is to toss the bread in a bowl with the oil. It's not imperitive that all of the bread is covered in oil, you just want the oil as spread out as possible. Toast bread until outer edges are a light golden brown around the edges.
